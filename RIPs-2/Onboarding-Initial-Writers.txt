Onboarding Incentives for Content Creators for October 

 

For the month of the October, for the launch of RECAP powered content sharing site buzzernepal.com | coindesknepal.com, 
we will be incentivizing the content creators by providing the  RECAP coin. For the onboarding purpose the half of the coin allocated for the development task of the  RECAP coin will be used which equates to 28,000. For the first 100 content creators the 28,000 coins will be distributed making each article worth 280 RECAP coins.
